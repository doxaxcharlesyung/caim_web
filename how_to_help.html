How to contribute

Support CAIM

Help CAIM expand training, consulting, and ministry tools while keeping the site and content fully self-contained.

Ways to help

  • Pray for the churches and leaders using CAIM resources.
  • Support translation, editing, and content preparation.
  • Contribute technical or ministry expertise to the project.
  • Help test the CAIM pages and report layout or content issues.

Current boundary

CAIM content stays on the CAIM site. Nothing on this page should point visitors outside the CAIM experience.
