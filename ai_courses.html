This page replicates the bottom-half course content from the source CAIM practical AI course page, translated into English for the CAIM English site.

MT101 - Comprehensive Church AI Applications Workshop

Helps pastors and church leaders understand the latest AI developments, including AI image generation, translation, video, music, administration, and office automation systems.

The workshop also introduces the foundations of AI theology and ethics, and demonstrates how DX Sermon can support deeper research and reflective ministry materials.

Learn More

MT102 - Second Brain: NotebookLM for Research and Ministry, Foundations

A hands-on introduction to Google's AI notebook and research assistant, NotebookLM, for Bible study, topic research, and course preparation.

Participants learn how to use large source collections to create practical audio summaries, custom reports, and presentation materials with greater efficiency and depth.

Learn More

MT103 - Second Brain: NotebookLM for Research and Ministry, Advanced

The advanced NotebookLM course covers custom report formats, higher-quality source libraries, managing different source types, advanced prompt-group setup, and cross-source comparison.

It also explores how NotebookLM can work with other AI tools so participants can use AI more effectively for research and ministry preparation.

Learn More

MT110 - Responsible Stewardship: Setting Church AI Use Policies

Helps church leaders respond to the opportunities and challenges brought by artificial intelligence. Through surveys and discussion, participants develop a full-church AI use framework.

The course emphasizes ethical foundations, basic legal awareness, and mature theological judgment so technology can serve people responsibly.
